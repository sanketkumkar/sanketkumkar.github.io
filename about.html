About Me

Hello! I'm Sanket Kumkar, a passionate cybersecurity enthusiast currently pursuing my BCA in Cyber Forensics & Information Security at Ajeenkya DY Patil University, Pune.

I've earned certifications in various cybersecurity domains and continuously expand my knowledge through real-world experience. When I’m not studying or learning, I actively participate in bug bounty hunting on platforms like HackerOne and Bugcrowd, where I explore vulnerabilities and contribute to making the internet a safer place.

Whether it’s diving into penetration testing, reverse engineering, or forensic analysis, I enjoy every bit of solving complex cyber puzzles. My mission is to grow as a cybersecurity expert and help secure the digital world.
