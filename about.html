About Me

Hey! I'm Sanket Kumkar, a passionate cybersecurity enthusiast currently pursuing my BCA in Cyber Forensics and Information Security at Ajeenkya DY Patil University.

While most students are still figuring out their paths, I’ve already found my spark in the digital shadows. My curiosity about how things break (and how to fix them) has led me to earn certifications across multiple cybersecurity domains — from ethical hacking to network defense.

Beyond academics, I actively engage in bug bounty hunting on platforms like HackerOne and Bugcrowd, where I enjoy identifying real-world vulnerabilities and making the web a safer place. Every exploit I find fuels my drive and sharpens my skills.

I blend my technical expertise with a constant hunger to learn, adapt, and innovate in the ever-evolving world of cybersecurity. Whether it’s dissecting malware, tracing digital footprints, or testing web apps — I’m all in.

This is just the beginning of my journey. The mission? To leave a secure mark on the digital world. 💻🔐
